## Getting Started with Wasabi

**1. Create a Wasabi Account:**

Visit [Wasabi’s website](https://wasabi.com), click “Start Free Trial” or “Sign Up,” and follow the registration steps. Confirm your email and set up your account.

**2. Log in to the Wasabi Console:**

Access the Wasabi Management Console at [console.wasabi.com](https://console.wasabi.com) using your credentials.

**3. Create a Bucket:**

* Navigate to the “**Buckets**” tab and click “**Create Bucket.**”
* Provide a unique bucket name and select a region (e.g., us-east-1).
* Click “**Create**” to finalize the bucket setup.

***

## Setting Up Access Keys for Wasabi

**1. Navigate to the Access Keys Section:**

* In the Wasabi Console, click on your profile in the top-right corner and choose “**Access Keys**” from the dropdown.

**2. Create an Access Key:**

* Click “**Create New Access Key**.”
* Choose whether the key is for a specific user or account-wide access.

**3. Generate Credentials:**

* Once created, copy the **Access Key** and **Secret Key** or download them securely.
* Keep the keys safe, as the Secret Key will not be shown again.
