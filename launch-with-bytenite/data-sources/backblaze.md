## Getting Started with Backblaze B2

**1. Create a Backblaze Account:**

Visit [Backblaze](https://www.backblaze.com), click “**Sign Up**,” and register for an account. You can start with a free trial or choose a plan.

**2. Log in to the Backblaze Console:**

Access your account at [backblaze.com](https://www.backblaze.com) using your credentials.

**3. Create a Bucket:**

* Navigate to the “**Buckets**” section in the left-hand menu.
* Click “C**reate a Bucket.**”
* Provide a bucket name, choose visibility (public or private), and click “**Create**.”

***

## Setting Up S3-Compatible Access Keys for Backblaze

**1. Enable S3-Compatible APIs:**

• Go to the “**Buckets**” section and ensure S3-compatible APIs are enabled for your account.

**2. Generate an Application Key:**

* In the Backblaze Console, go to “**App Keys**” under the “**Buckets**” menu.
* Click “**Add a New Application Key**.”
* Specify a name and optional bucket restrictions for the key.

**3. Retrieve Your Keys:**

* Once created, Backblaze will display your **Key ID** and **Application Key**.
* Save these credentials securely, as the Application Key will not be displayed again.
